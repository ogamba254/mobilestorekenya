01

Nairobi Express

Orders placed before 2 PM are delivered within 3-6 hours. Delivery fee is a flat KSh 300 within the CBD and KSh 500 for surrounding estates (Runda, Karen, Langata, etc.).

02

Countrywide Shipping

We partner with G4S, Wells Fargo, and reliable shuttles for deliveries outside Nairobi. Delivery typically takes 24-48 hours. Shipping costs vary by location and package size.

03

Order Tracking

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a tracking number via SMS and Email to monitor your package in real-time.
