Warranty Shield.

1. Local Warranty Coverage

All new smartphones and laptops carry a 1-Year Limited Manufacturer Warranty. This covers hardware failure and factory defects. We only sell official East African stock, meaning your warranty is valid at any authorized service center in Kenya.

2. What is Not Covered

  • Physical damage (Broken screens, dents, or bent frames).
  • Liquid damage (Even for water-resistant rated devices).
  • Software modification (Rooting, Jailbreaking, or unofficial ROMs).

7-Day Return

Factory defects reported within 7 days qualify for a direct replacement or refund.

Save Receipt

Your digital or physical receipt is your primary warranty document. Do not lose it.
